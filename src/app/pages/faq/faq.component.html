General Enquiries


Do you have a physical store?

We welcome you to visit our showroom at Tsuen Wan, where you can try on our bags, and understand more about our design and quality. You can also purchase directly from our showroom and enjoy free gift-wrapping service. Please call for appointment.

Showroom

Where can I buy your products?

Apart from online shop and showroom, you can also find our designs at Zalora HK and Jill Scott HK retail stores.

Can I pick up the goods directly from showroom?

Yes! By choosing self pick-up at check out, you will receive an email and an sms about pick up time and address. We accept cash, Visa/Master card and Wechat payment at showroom. Please note that unpaid goods will be reserved for you for one month upon pick-up notification.

Where can I check your latest promotions and offers?

Welcome to subscribe our newsletters and follow us on social media platforms ( Facebook, Instagram ) for latest promotions and offers.

How do I change my shipping address or cancel order after placing order?

For any change of order, please contact our customer service team by email or telephone, and we would be glad to solve your problems. Please kindly note that order details cannot be changed once goods are shipped.
