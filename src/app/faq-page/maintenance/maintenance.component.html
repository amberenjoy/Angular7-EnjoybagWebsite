We provide a half year of free maintenance service since the order date, including replacement of defective parts to the product, for which is under maintenance period and normal use.

Maintenance service does not cover man-made damage and natural deterioration of leather, such as stains, rotten or natural loss of leather.

After free maintenance period, you are welcomed to contact us regarding product repair service. Our team will evaluate product condition and propose repair fee correspondingly.

It may take 4 to 6 weeks to complete whole product maintenance service.
