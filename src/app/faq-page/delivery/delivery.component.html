Delivery & Return


Do you provide free shipping to Hong Kong and Mainland China ?

Yes, we provide free delivery to Hong Kong and Mainland China standard addresses for order over specified amount, standard delivery fee will be charged for order below amount. Once your parcel is dispatched, you will receive an email and an sms with tracking details.

Do you provide international shipping?

For delivery outside Hong Kong and China, customers are responsible for the shipping fees and incurred service charge.

Shipping fees shown in check-out is for reference only. The actual fee might be varied by special conditions and customs of delivery country. We will notify customers by email the most accurate fee after receiving order.

Please note that products ordered by customers are subject to restrictions on the import regulations of the delivery country and customer agrees to comply with all applicable entry regulations.

The customer understands that the import regulations or restrictions on the products ordered will change due to time and will lead to delays in delivery.

The customer is either responsible for the import duties and customs clearance fees and the payment of all local sales and VAT.

Once your parcel is dispatched, you will receive an email and a sms with tracking details.

Do you have free returns?

You can return any non-sale goods for a refund/exchange within 14 days of purchase.

Please provide your original invoice when you return/exchange the item and make sure that goods are in re-saleable condition: unused, undamaged and with its original packaging, such as barcode stickers, dust bags and so on.

We reserve the rights of not refunding/exchanging purchased items given that it does not meet the re-saleable condition. Goods will be returned by standard post.

For other delivery methods, additional shipping charge might be applied to customer.

Discount items are non-refundable.
